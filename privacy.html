Privacy Policy

Shopease Foods Inc. · Last updated June 2026

Shopease Foods Inc. ("Shopease", "we") respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect, why, and how we handle it, in accordance with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL).

What we collect

  • Contact details — name, phone, email, address and postal code, when you fill out our survey, book a consult, or place an order.
  • Household and food preferences — what you tell us in the survey or a consult (household size, grocery budget, items you like) so we can build your plan.
  • Order and payment details — the contents of your orders, delivery details, and payment arrangements. Payment card and banking details are handled through our payment and financing partners; we do not store full card numbers on our website.

How we use it

  • To prepare your quote, plan your order, schedule deliveries and provide customer service.
  • To send you messages about your inquiry and order (with your consent where required).
  • To send marketing offers only if you opted in — you can withdraw that consent at any time by replying STOP to any text or using the unsubscribe link in any email.

Who we share it with

  • Financing partners — only when you apply for financing, and only the information needed for your application.
  • Delivery partners — name, address and phone, only as needed to deliver your order.
  • Service providers — systems we use to run our business (scheduling, messaging, e-signature), bound to use your information only on our behalf.

We do not sell your personal information. Ever.

Retention and security

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes above and to meet legal and accounting requirements, and we protect it with appropriate technical and organizational safeguards.
